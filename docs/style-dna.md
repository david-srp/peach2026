# Style DNA — Peach 2026

> Flat 2D children's illustration inspired by **Bluey (Ludo Studio)** art direction.

## Shape Language
- Dominated by **circles and rounded rectangles** — no sharp angles or triangles
- All proportions are **miniaturized and chunky**, like dollhouse toys

## Linework
- Never black
- Outlines match a **darker, more saturated version** of the object's local color

## Lighting & Shadows
- Warm and golden, evoking **late-afternoon subtropical sun**
- All shadows carry **color bias**:
  - Blue objects → purple shadows
  - Orange objects → warm brown shadows
  - **Never neutral gray**

## Background
- Flat and graphic with **minimal perspective**
- 2-3 layered color planes to suggest depth

## Texture
- Subtle **watercolor paper grain**, not smooth vector

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary warm | Orange | `#E27A37` |
| Primary cool | Sky Blue | `#88CAFC` |
| Neutral base | Cream | `#E4DCBD` |
| Accent warm | Golden Yellow | `#EDCC6F` |
| Supporting 1 | Soft Lavender | — |
| Supporting 2 | Mint | — |

## Mood
Warm, cozy, playful, safe.

## Target
Preschool-age children (3-5 years old).
